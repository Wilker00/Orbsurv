Quick Start

Getting Started Guide

Follow these steps to get your Orbsurv system up and running.

1

Unbox and Inspect

Unbox your Orbsurv Patrol Module and rail segments. Verify all components are present: module, rail segments, mounting brackets, power supply, and installation hardware. Check for any damage and contact support if anything is missing.

2

Plan Your Installation

Determine the best location for your rail system. Consider coverage area, mounting surface, power availability, and network connectivity. Use the Orbsurv app's planning tool to visualize coverage before installation.

3

Install Rail System

Mount the rail segments using the snap-fit brackets. Ensure the rail is level and securely fastened. Connect rail segments together, making sure connections are secure. Route cables through the hidden cable channel.

4

Mount and Connect Module

Attach the Patrol Module to the rail. Connect PoE++ power and network cable, or configure Wi-Fi/5G connectivity. Ensure the module moves smoothly along the rail. Power on and wait for initialization.

5

Download and Setup App

Download the Orbsurv app from the App Store or Google Play. Create an account or sign in. Follow the in-app setup wizard to connect your module, configure network settings, and complete initial calibration.

6

Calibrate and Configure

Run the automatic calibration process. The system will map your space and learn optimal patrol routes. Configure detection zones, privacy settings, and alert preferences. Set up patrol schedules for different times of day.

Solutions

Common Issues & Solutions

Quick fixes for the most common problems

Connection Issues

Can't connect to your module?

  • Verify PoE++ power supply is connected and working
  • Check network cable connection and Wi-Fi signal strength
  • Restart the module through the app or power cycle

False Alarms

Getting too many false alerts?

  • Adjust detection sensitivity in app settings
  • Create exclusion zones for pets or moving objects
  • Let AI learn your space for 2-3 days to improve accuracy

Rail Movement Issues

Camera not moving smoothly?

  • Check rail alignment and ensure it's level
  • Clean rail surface and remove any obstructions
  • Recalibrate the system through the app
