About Orbsurv

We're building rail-mounted, AI-assisted surveillance that patrols your space, eliminates blind spots, and alerts you only when it matters. Reduce cameras, reduce noise, increase signal.

Our Mission

We provide a smarter, privacy-first way to keep spaces safe. Orbsurv learns daily patterns, combining motion and thermal data to highlight anomalies.

How It Works

  1. The rail robot patrols configurable routes.
  2. On-device AI builds baseline activity patterns.
  3. Sensor fusion reduces false positives.
  4. Events route to mobile and web in real time.

Yes. We combine video with thermal sensing for reliable detection.
