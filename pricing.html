Pricing questions

What's included in the monthly fee?

Each plan includes hardware leasing (rail segments, camera module, power supply), annual preventive maintenance, overnight replacement if a rail segment fails, cloud storage, AI processing, and software updates. No additional setup or installation fees for Starter and Perimeter plans.

What is the contract commitment?

Starter and Perimeter plans require a 12-month commitment. Enterprise plans offer flexible terms based on deployment scale. Early termination fees may apply�contact us for details.

Can I add more coverage later?

Yes. Additional rails and sensors are billed per foot. Enterprise customers can expand to new sites with volume pricing. Changes to your plan can be made at renewal.

Do you offer pilots or trials?

The Starter tier is ideal for pilots. We also support custom proof-of-concept deployments�reach out via the contact form to discuss trial options.
