Frequently Asked Questions

What is the required power supply?

The system requires standard 24V DC power, typically consuming 60W during normal operation and peaking at 120W during high-speed rail movement and continuous recording.

Is the rail system suitable for harsh environments?

Yes. The rail is constructed from reinforced aluminum with self-lubricating polymer components, designed to withstand high winds, dust, and continuous moisture exposure per its IP67 rating.

How is the data stored and accessed?

Critical data clips and metadata are stored locally on hardened, encrypted solid-state drives. Access is provided via a secure local network API, with optional integration into existing VMS systems via ONVIF protocol.
