Pricing questions

Is hardware included?

Each plan includes hardware leasing, annual preventative maintenance, and overnight replacement if a rail segment fails.

Can I add more coverage later?

Yes. Additional rails and sensors are billed per foot. Enterprise customers can expand to new sites with volume pricing.

Do you offer pilots?

The Starter tier is ideal for pilots. We also support custom proof-of-concept deployments - reach out via the contact form.
