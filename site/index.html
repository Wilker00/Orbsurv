How Orbsurv works

Rail movement

Motorized track gives panoramic coverage and eliminates blind spots, even in corners and long corridors.

AI events

On-device detection for people, vehicles, pets, heat and motion with smart filtering to cut false alerts.

Privacy first

Local processing by default, with selective blur and redaction for shared clips.

Reliable by design
