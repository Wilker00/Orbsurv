The Problem

Traditional fixed cameras are failing to provide adequate security in modern spaces.

  • Blind Spots: Static viewpoints mean large areas are left unmonitored.
  • High Costs: Multiple cameras are needed for full coverage, increasing hardware and installation costs.
  • Reactive, Not Proactive: Most systems only alert you after an event has already occurred.
  • False Alarms: Basic motion detection triggers on shadows, pets, and environmental changes.
  • Privacy Concerns: Cloud-based processing raises data security and privacy issues.

The Solution

Orbsurv is a singular, intelligent system that actively monitors your entire space.

  • Panoramic Coverage: One camera moves to eliminate blind spots.
  • Smart AI: On-device processing detects people, pets, and vehicles while ignoring "noise".
  • Learning & Adapting: The system learns your space and patterns to improve over time.
  • Privacy First: Local processing by default, with optional cloud features.
  • Cost Effective: One system replaces multiple fixed cameras.

Product Snapshot

A sleek, modular rail system with an autonomous AI camera unit.

  • What: A camera on a motorized rail.
  • Who: Homeowners and small business owners.
  • Why: To provide complete, intelligent, and affordable surveillance.
