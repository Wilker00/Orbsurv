Warranty & Returns

Comprehensive warranty coverage and hassle-free returns for your peace of mind

Warranty Coverage

All Orbsurv hardware comes with a comprehensive warranty covering manufacturing defects and component failures.

  • Duration: 2 years from date of purchase
  • Coverage: Hardware defects, component failures, and manufacturing issues
  • Service: Free repair or replacement of defective components
  • Support: Priority technical support during warranty period

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your purchase.

  • Timeframe: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Product must be in original packaging with all accessories
  • Process: Contact support to initiate return authorization
  • Refund: Full refund within 5-7 business days after receipt

Warranty Claims Process

1

Contact Support

Reach out to our support team via email at support@orbsurv.com or through your account dashboard. Provide your order number and a description of the issue.

2

Receive RMA Number

Our team will review your claim and issue a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number if the issue is covered under warranty. You'll receive instructions for returning the device.

3

Ship Device

Package the device securely with the RMA number clearly visible. We provide prepaid shipping labels for warranty claims. Ship to our service center using the provided label.

4

Repair or Replacement

Our technicians will inspect and repair your device, or provide a replacement if repair is not possible. Most repairs are completed within 5-7 business days.

5

Return Shipment

Once repaired or replaced, we'll ship your device back to you at no cost. You'll receive tracking information and can expect delivery within 3-5 business days.

What's Not Covered

The warranty does not cover damage caused by:

  • Accidental damage, misuse, or abuse
  • Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from environmental factors (water, extreme temperatures, etc.)
  • Loss or theft
  • Software issues not related to hardware defects
