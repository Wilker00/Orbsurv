Core Capabilities

Rail-mounted mechanics, fused sensing, and onboard AI keep operators ahead of critical events.

Rail Intelligence

The motorized mounting system executes pre-programmed sweep patterns and adapts its speed and focus dynamically based on monitored events.

Sensor Fusion

Combines high-definition visual imagery with thermal and high-frequency motion sensing data streams for comprehensive awareness, day or night.

Onboard Learning

Edge computing runs proprietary AI models to learn baseline behavior, dramatically reducing false alarms and only alerting on anomalous movement.
