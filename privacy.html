Privacy & Protection

Your data, under your control.

Orbsurv is built for safety, not surveillance. We minimize what we collect, encrypt everything, and give you clear controls to export or delete your data. This page explains exactly what we collect, what we don't, and your rights under GDPR/CCPA.

See your controls Read compliance

What we collect

Video & snapshots

Event-based video frames and short clips around anomalies (not continuous 24/7 storage by default).

Event metadata

Timestamps, rail zone, motion/heat scores, and non-PII descriptors like clothing color/patterns for re-identification when faces aren’t used.

Device health

Diagnostics like firmware version, uptime, and connectivity to keep your system secure and updated.

What we don't do

No selling data

We never sell your data to advertisers or brokers. Full stop.

No sharing without consent

We don't share outside your account unless you explicitly authorize it (e.g., share a clip).

No private-zone capture

Areas you mask (windows, neighbor yards, bathrooms) are not recorded or processed.

Your control

Export & portability

Download your videos, events, and metadata in portable formats. Ideal for audits or migration.

Deletion on demand

Erase some or all stored content, including backups, with verifiable deletion receipts.

Privacy zones & retention

Mask out sensitive areas and choose how long events are retained per zone or globally.

Encryption & access

End-to-end encryption in transit and at rest. Role-based access, device-bound sessions, and signed URLs for shared clips.

Compliance

GDPR (EU)

We honor data subject rights (access, rectification, deletion, portability, restriction, objection). EU data can be region‑pinned.

CCPA/CPRA (California)

Right to know, delete, and opt‑out of sale/share. We provide a simple portal to submit requests and view status.

Security posture

Least‑privilege keys, rotating secrets, audit logs, and regular third‑party penetration tests. SOC‑2 is on our roadmap.

Questions or requests? Email privacy@orbsurv.com. For urgent matters, see the in‑app Privacy tab → Help.
