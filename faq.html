General Questions

How is Orbsurv different from static cameras?

Orbsurv patrols on a rail, eliminating blind spots that fixed cameras create. Instead of installing multiple static cameras, a single Orbsurv module moves along a track to provide comprehensive coverage. It also learns your space over time, reducing false alarms and improving detection accuracy.

Does Orbsurv work in low light?

Yes. Orbsurv combines high-resolution video with thermal sensing for reliable detection in any lighting condition. The thermal sensor detects heat signatures even in complete darkness, while the 4K camera provides clear footage during daylight hours.

Where is my data processed?

Events are analyzed on-device first using edge AI processing. This means your surveillance data is processed locally, maintaining privacy and reducing bandwidth usage. Cloud syncing is optional and can be configured to your preferences. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Does the mobile app run on iOS and Android?

Yes, the Orbsurv mobile app is built with Flutter and available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. You can monitor your space, receive alerts, and review footage from anywhere.

What is the range of the rail system?

Each rail segment is 2 meters long, and multiple segments can be connected. The standard deployment supports up to 50 meters of rail coverage. Custom installations can extend beyond this for larger facilities.

Installation

How difficult is installation?

Installation is straightforward. The modular rail system uses snap-fit brackets that mount to your ceiling or wall. Most installations take 2-4 hours for a standard deployment. We provide detailed installation guides and video tutorials, and white-glove installation services are available for complex deployments.

What are the hardware requirements?

Orbsurv requires 90W PoE++ (Power over Ethernet Plus Plus) for power and data. The system can also operate with Wi-Fi 6E or optional 5G connectivity. A network connection is required for app access and optional cloud features. See our Installation Guide for complete specifications.

Can I install it myself or do I need a professional?

Many users successfully install Orbsurv themselves using our installation guide. However, for optimal performance and calibration, we recommend professional installation, especially for complex deployments or commercial spaces. Pilot participants receive installation support.

How long does calibration take?

Initial calibration typically takes 15-30 minutes. The system automatically maps your space and learns optimal patrol routes. Advanced calibration with custom zones and rules can take 1-2 hours depending on complexity.

Configuration

How do I set up patrol routes?

Patrol routes are configured through the Orbsurv app or web interface. You can set waypoints, define dwell times at specific locations, and create schedules for different times of day. The system learns from your patterns and suggests optimizations.

Can I customize privacy zones?

Yes. Orbsurv supports privacy-first redaction policies. You can define zones where faces or sensitive areas are automatically blurred in recordings while still allowing the system to detect motion and anomalies in those areas.

How do I configure alerts and notifications?

Alerts are configured through the app. You can set up motion detection zones, define what types of events trigger alerts, and customize notification preferences. The edge AI filters out false alarms, so you only receive alerts for meaningful events.

Can I integrate Orbsurv with other security systems?

Yes. Orbsurv provides API access for integration with existing security systems, access control, and building management platforms. Webhook support is available for real-time event notifications. See our Developer Documentation for integration guides.

Troubleshooting

Why am I getting false alarms?

False alarms are typically caused by environmental factors like moving shadows, reflections, or pets. The edge AI learns your space over time to reduce false alarms. You can also adjust sensitivity settings, configure exclusion zones, and fine-tune detection parameters in the app. If issues persist, contact support.

The camera isn't moving smoothly on the rail.

Check that the rail is properly aligned and all segments are securely connected. Ensure the rail is clean and free of obstructions. If the issue persists, the module may need recalibration. See our Help Center for detailed troubleshooting steps or contact support.

I'm having connectivity issues.

First, verify your network connection and PoE power supply. Check that the module's LED indicators show normal operation. Try rebooting the module through the app. If connectivity problems persist, ensure your network meets the requirements (PoE++, Wi-Fi 6E, or 5G) and contact support for assistance.

How do I reset the system to factory defaults?

A factory reset can be performed through the app settings under "Advanced" → "Reset to Factory Defaults". This will erase all configurations, patrol routes, and learned patterns. You'll need to recalibrate after resetting. Warning: This action cannot be undone.
