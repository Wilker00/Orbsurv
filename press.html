About Orbsurv

Orbsurv is an intelligent surveillance system that combines rail-mounted cameras with on-device AI to eliminate blind spots and reduce false alarms. Our mission is to provide smarter, privacy-first security solutions that learn normal activity patterns and alert only when it matters.
