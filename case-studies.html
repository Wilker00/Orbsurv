Warehouse Security Transformation

A large distribution center reduced camera count by 70% while improving coverage and reducing false alarms.

Industry Logistics & Distribution
Location 500,000 sq ft facility
Deployment 12 Orbsurv units
Timeline 3 months

Challenge

The facility previously used 40 static cameras, resulting in numerous blind spots, high maintenance costs, and an overwhelming number of false alarms from normal warehouse activity. Security staff spent significant time reviewing footage that turned out to be routine operations.

Solution

Orbsurv units were strategically placed along main aisles and high-traffic areas. The AI system learned normal activity patterns over the first month, dramatically reducing false positives. Patrol routes were configured to cover critical zones during peak hours.

Retail Chain Perimeter Security

A retail chain deployed Orbsurv across 15 locations to enhance perimeter security and reduce theft.

Industry Retail
Scale 15 locations
Deployment 2 units per location
Timeline 6 months rollout

Challenge

The retail chain experienced increasing incidents of after-hours break-ins and perimeter violations. Static cameras at each location had blind spots, and monitoring 15 locations required significant resources.

Solution

Two Orbsurv units per location were installed to cover loading docks and main entrances. Centralized monitoring through the cloud dashboard allowed security teams to monitor all locations from a single interface. Thermal detection helped identify activity even in low-light conditions.
